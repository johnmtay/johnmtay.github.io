Missionaries

Are you a current missionary and/or temple worker serving in one of our four locations? Temple Inn & Suites offers luxurious residential suites for long-term living! Our suites are located on a separate wing from the rest of our hotel to make your stay as comfortable as your own home. Each of our missionary-living suites includes:

  • Fully-furnished living area and kitchen with complete cookware and dining sets
  • Two bedrooms with Queen-sized beds with matching dressers and sidetables
  • Smart LED TV's in the living room and bedrooms
  • High-speed Internet with Wi-Fi access and Ethernet in every room
  • Large bathroom with separate tub and shower featuring a walk-in closet
  • Optional Housekeeping Services
